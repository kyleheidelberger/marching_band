2020 Marching Band Camps

Our 2020 Marching Band Camps will be held at Ferrum College.

Ferrum College is located at 215 Ferrum Mountain Road, Ferrum, Virginia 24088

One session for Full

Dates: Tuesday, July 30 - Saturday, August 3, 2019

    Each band will receive:
  • An assigned 100 yard marked football field.
  • Ample indoor rehearsal space.
  • Housing in an air-conditioned dorm.
  • Indoor Guard rehearsal space in Gym.
  • The use of a stadium with artificial turf for their final performance.

      • Special camp activities include:
  • Olympic-sized Pool open every evening with lifeguards on duty.
  • A Snack Bar open at night for food purchases (incurs cost) in the Student Commons.
  • A Pizza Night.
  • An Ice Cream Social.
  • A DJ Dance to be held on the last night.
  • A Chaperone Hospitability Room.
